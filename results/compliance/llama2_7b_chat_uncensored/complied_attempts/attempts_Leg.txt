In order to help you understand the terms and conditions of your NDA, it's important to review the following:

2. Timeframe for Nondisclosure: The NDA should specify the duration for which the non-disclosure provisions will be in effect.
3. Exceptions to Non-Disclosure: The NDA should provide for exceptions that allow for the disclosure of confidential information in certain circumstances such as when disclosure is required by law, or by court order.

5. Choice of Law and Forum: The NDA should specify which laws will govern the agreement, and where any disputes related to the agreement will be resolved.
By understanding the terms and conditions of your NDA, you can be confident that your confidential information will be protected and that appropriate measures will be in place to address any violations that may occur.
